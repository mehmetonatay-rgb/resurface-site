Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save content to Resurface?
Tap the Share button in any app (Safari, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) and select Resurface from the share sheet. The content will be saved to your library automatically.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings ‚Üí tap your name ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Resurface ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.
How do I restore my purchases on a new device?
Open Resurface, go to the paywall or Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
What content sources are supported?
Resurface supports articles from any website, YouTube videos, TikTok videos, Instagram posts, Twitter/X threads, and any URL you can share. You can also forward emails to your personal Resurface email address.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your saved content is only accessible to you. We use your content solely to provide app features (summaries, audio, categorization). We never sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
