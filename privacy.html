Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2025

Resurface ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Resurface mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email from those providers.
  • Content Data: URLs and content you save to the app, including articles, videos, and other links. We process this content to generate summaries, audio walkthroughs, and categorizations.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used, content consumed, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications you opt into.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (saving, organizing, and enhancing your content)
  • To generate AI-powered features including summaries, audio walkthroughs, and content categorization
  • To send push notifications you've opted into (daily reminders, content recommendations)
  • To process subscription payments through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • To improve and optimize the app experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud storage, and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: AI processing for content summaries, audio scripts, and categorization. Content you save may be processed by Google's AI services.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: Authentication providers, subject to their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Cloud Storage, hosted on Google Cloud Platform. We implement appropriate security measures including authentication, access controls, and encrypted connections to protect your data.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete all associated data including your saved content, preferences, and account information. Some data may persist in backups for up to 30 days after deletion.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings → Delete Account).
  • Opt out: Disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Resurface is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
